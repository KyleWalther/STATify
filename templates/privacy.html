Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3/16/2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to STATify! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use STATify:

  • Spotify Data: We access your Spotify account data, including your playlists, top tracks, artists, and albums, to provide personalized insights.
  • Authentication Data: We use Spotify's OAuth process to authenticate users. We do not store your Spotify credentials; they are handled securely by Spotify's authorization system.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing personalized music insights based on your Spotify account data.
  • Improving the functionality and performance of STATify.
  • Enhancing your user experience by offering relevant data analytics.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except as necessary to provide the services offered by STATify. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We do not store any personal information related to your Spotify account. All data is temporarily cached during your session for the purpose of generating your insights. Once your session ends, all data is deleted from our servers.

6. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we collect. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us via email at kylerickwalther@yahoo.com.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
